Own a Franchise


Why should you invest in WellFit Gyms?

Why should you invest in a fitness studio in general? Fitness is a lifestyle, not a hobby or a passing trend. This is why unlike many other businesses, gyms are relatively unaffected by seasonal changes. It is rare to run a business that actually makes a real, positive difference in people's lives. A gym is one of those rare businesses. In the gym people become fitter, healthier, happier and more confident—and you can actually see those things unfold. Who wouldn't want to run a business like that?

Careers at Wellfit


Our Mission

Considering a career in fitness. Wellfit is the place to be! We at Wellfit want to help people recognize the importance of fitness and show them how to adopt fitness regimens that will dramatically improve the quality of their lives. We seek dynamic and passionate personnel for this and to meet our rapid expansion needs. The opportunities for growth are tremendous, with more clubs opening.


Why Wellfit?

Across our clubs, our members feel Wellfit to be their second home as we strive to keep consistency in the look and feel of all our clubs by maintaining the world-class facilities and offering a high standard of service. Some of the facilities offered include gym, studio, 1:1 Personal Training Counter, cardio theater, cycling studio, members lounge and luxorious changing rooms.

Taking your career to the next level

We give our team the opportunity to get more out of their career by inspiring each other to learn new things, achieve new qualifications, and get fitter together. We recognise talent and support our team to go further in their career. From taking advantage of our global opportunities to having the confidence to inspire our members, we encourage our team to raise the bar so we can celebrate and reward their great results.

Helping you go further

We’re committed to inspiring our members to get fitter as we believe this helps them build confidence to get more out of life.

Fun and inspiring enviornments

We work together as one team in environments full of inspiration, innovation and fun!

The opportunity to lead a fitter lifestyle

We encourage everyone to work out. It helps us feel great and go further in life. As an employee, you get free membership and a discounted one for a friend/family member.

World-class training and development

Our people count. That’s why we offer ongoing development from the day you join to build a team of fitness experts.
